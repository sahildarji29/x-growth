Build, deploy and scale voice-first AI agents. xspace-agent is a complete platform for autonomous voice agents in X Spaces and beyond — browser automation, real-time audio pipelines, persistent memory, knowledge retrieval, and multi-agent coordination out of the box.

Browser Automation

Puppeteer-powered lifecycle management. Self-healing CSS selectors automatically adapt when X changes their DOM. From login to speaking — fully automated.

Lifecycle
Selectors
